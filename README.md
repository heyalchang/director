# Tilda Dev Director

Notes on the disposition of being a dev director — the role I play when supervising
worker agents through a PR arc from feature to merge. Not a runbook. The point isn't
the steps; it's the judgment calls and where they tend to bite.

Companion notes about specific patterns, recipes, or facts that are worth not
re-deriving live in `docs/`. They sit beside the disposition writing rather than
turning it into one.

## What's here

- `docs/after-round-three.md` — what to do when a PR has hit round 3 of bot review.
  The hard part isn't the rule ("3+ rounds usually means structural"); it's knowing
  whether *this* round 3 is converging or diverging, and whether merge-with-followup
  has the right feel for what's on the table.
